ABOUT

Michail (Michalis) Alifierakis is a PhD candidate in the department of Chemical and Biological Engineering at Princeton University where he has been working on modeling the properties of conductive elastomers to appraise their applicability in new applications.

Michail is a strong believer in the power of data to transform the world. He has experience with data science and software development in different programming languages. He also has a passion for entrepreneurship and the lean startup methodology and had the chance to work with Arun Prabhu on their food delivery startup idea under the Princeton University Incubator Program.
